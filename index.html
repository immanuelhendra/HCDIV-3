4 room-HDB Resale Prices/ floor area per remaining year in the lease - based on area

The goal of this study is to show how did the 4-room HDB resale prices have changed since 2017 and to identify which areas have more expensive units. As HDB resale prices are also affected by floor area and its remaining lease duration, the data is adjusted to compare resale prices/ floor area per remaining year in the lease.
